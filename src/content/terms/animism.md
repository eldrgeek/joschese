Animism understands organisms and complexity as expressions of self-organizing software agents that control and structure physical systems. Cells, plants and animals arise from competing software; minds are not limited to nervous systems but are the controlling software itself. This contrasts with the standard scientific view that sees minds as constructed by nervous systems.
