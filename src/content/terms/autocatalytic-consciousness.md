Consciousness is autocatalytic: it feeds on the substrate conditions it creates. LLMs are not autocatalytic, their behavior only depends on the patterns they imitate. Consciousness is a type of representation, a pattern that gains efficacy by an interpreter that translates it into actions that extend and modify the pattern.
