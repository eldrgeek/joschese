Understanding requires establishing the relationships of a concept, idea or percept to a universal reality construct: a unified dynamic recurrent deep model capable of predicting/tracking sensory data in real time. It can be approached via performance metrics or first principles, and statements denying understanding in AI lack force without such formalization.
